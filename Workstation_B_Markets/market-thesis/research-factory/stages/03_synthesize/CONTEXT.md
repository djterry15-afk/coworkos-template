# 03_synthesize — Evidence → Brief (L2 stage contract)
*One job: assemble the graded evidence into the research brief. The **alignment gate** — does
the brief faithfully represent the evidence, and stop short of a verdict?*

---

## Contract

| | |
|---|---|
| **Input** | `02_extract/evidence-<slug>.md` |
| **Also load** | `_config/artifact-format.md` |
| **Skip** | the raw snapshot (work from extracted evidence) · other runs · the thesis synthesis |
| **Output** | `03_synthesize/output/brief-<slug>.md` |

---

## Process

1. Write the brief to the `artifact-format.md` schema: Question · State of the evidence ·
   Evidence FOR · AGAINST/complicating · Contradictions & gaps · Source ledger · Verdict-deferred.
2. Every claim carries its graded source + provenance. **No claim without one.**
3. "State of the evidence" describes the **evidence's weight**, not the thesis.
4. If gate 01 surfaced a candidate angle, offer it under Verdict-deferred as *the agent's read* —
   never asserted.

---

## GATE → done

the operator confirms: the brief represents the graded evidence · contradictions aren't smoothed over ·
it stops short of a verdict. **On accept:** the operator reasons it into the parent market-thesis project `CONTEXT.md` (his
call) · sources optionally handed to the wiki · working files archived → `_archive/<slug>/`.

---

## What NOT to do

- Don't issue a verdict or recommendation. Evidence in, evidence out.
- Don't introduce a claim that isn't in the evidence file.
- Don't smooth contradictions — surfacing them is the value.

# Narrowing the Audience — Session Distillation
*(Example.) 2026-06-20. The session that set who the writing is for. Conclusion already lives in the project
`CONTEXT.md`; this is the trace behind it.*

---

## Question
Who are we writing for — and, as importantly, who are we *not* writing for?

## What was reasoned
The strategy was trying to speak to two audiences at once (practitioners who want depth, and newcomers who
want orientation), and the result read as neither. Picking one sharpens every later craft decision — voice,
examples, how much is assumed. The depth audience is the one the durable positioning can defend.

## Conclusion (flushed to CONTEXT.md)
Write for the practitioner who already has context and wants the non-obvious cut — not the newcomer. The
newcomer can follow; the piece isn't built for them. This is the positioning the factory's craft config
inherits.
